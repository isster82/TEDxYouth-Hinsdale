What is TEDxYouth@Hinsdale?

TEDxYouth@Hinsdale is a community event designed to foster inspiration, thought, and a desire to learn. Our special event will include a lineup of fascinating speakers and performers. Come out to TEDxYouth@Hinsdale for a once-in-a-lifetime, thought-provoking experience! This year's talks will center around the theme "Hidden in Plain Sight." More details to be announced.
